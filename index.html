Privacy Statement

Our Privacy Vow: We pledge to protect your data. While we must share incident details to fight cybercrime, your personal identity is treated with the utmost confidentiality.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly via our contact forms, including your name, email address, phone number, and organization name. Crucially, we also collect technical details regarding cybercrime incidents, which may include evidence such as screenshots, email headers, malicious URLs, and communication logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

For Individuals: We use your report data to investigate the incident, identify the responsible service providers (e.g., hosting companies, registrars), and file abuse reports. We may use anonymized, aggregate data to document these tactics and educate the public and authorities.

For Organizations: We use provided information to assess your security posture, identify active threats targeting your platform, and consult on internal Trust & Safety operations.

3. Information Sharing

To fulfill our mission of "Fortifying Digital Institutions," we must share specific incident details with third parties. This includes:

  • Service Providers: We report abuse to third-party hosting providers, domain registrars, and platforms hosting malicious content to request takedowns.
  • Law Enforcement & Government: We may share incident documentation with authorities to aid in the broader fight against cybercrime.

We do not sell your personal contact information to advertisers.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the data you entrust to the KelpDesk against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
